How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor lets you reduce the file size of your images while maintaining good quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values mean better quality but larger files)
  3. Choose your preferred output format (WebP is recommended for best results)
  4. Optionally resize your image to specific dimensions
  5. Click "Download" to save your compressed image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster websites: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience and SEO rankings
  • Reduced bandwidth usage: Save on hosting costs and data transfers
  • Better email delivery: Compressed images are more likely to bypass email size restrictions
  • Optimized social media: Some platforms compress your images automatically, often with poor results
